# ForcesAndMotion Deep Dive



Newton's Laws of Motion: Grade 8 Physics

Newton's Second Law of Motion states that the acceleration of an object is directly proportional to the net force acting on it and inversely proportional to its mass.

Arangkada ng Jeepney! (F = ma)

In this simulation, applying a larger 
Pwersa ng Makina (Engine Force)
 makes the jeepney accelerate faster. However, loading more 
Pasahero (Passengers/Mass)
 makes it much harder for the jeepney to speed up.
