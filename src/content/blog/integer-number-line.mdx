# IntegerNumberLine Deep Dive



Integers and Operations: Math Grade 7 (MATATAG)

An 
integer
 is any whole number, including negative numbers, zero, and positive numbers: ...−3, −2, −1, 0, 1, 2, 3...

Paano Gamitin ang Number Line

Addition of a Positive:
 Move to the 
RIGHT
 on the number line.

Addition of a Negative:
 Move to the 
LEFT
 on the number line.

Subtraction of a Positive:
 Move to the 
LEFT
 (opposite of addition).

Subtraction of a Negative:
 Move to the 
RIGHT
 (subtracting a negative is adding).

Bakit Mahalaga ang Integers?

Understanding integer operations is the 
absolute foundation
 for all algebra. If you cannot add and subtract negative numbers confidently, you cannot solve equations, work with coordinates, or understand scientific formulas. Every mathematical concept from Grade 8 onward depends on this skill.

Use the simulator above to see how the arrows move on the number line. Pay attention to the 
direction
 of movement — that's the key to understanding integer operations!
