# ElectromagneticSpectrum Deep Dive



Electromagnetic Radiation: Grade 10 Physics

The electromagnetic (EM) spectrum is the range of all types of EM radiation. Radiation is energy that travels and spreads out as it goes.

As you move from Radio Waves to Gamma Rays, the 
wavelength decreases
 while the 
frequency and energy increase
.

This is why Radio Waves safely pass through us every day, but high-energy Ultraviolet (UV) rays can cause sunburns during summer, requiring sunblock.
