Real-World Applications (Paggamit sa Tunay na Buhay)

Trigonometry is used to calculate the height of buildings, the distance across rivers, the angle of elevation when looking at a mountain peak, and even in navigation and engineering. Filipino surveyors use these ratios every day to measure land parcels and building heights.

Try setting the angle to 45° — notice how sin and cos are equal! At 30° and 60°, observe the classic ratios used throughout physics and engineering.
