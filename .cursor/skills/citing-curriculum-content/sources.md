# Vetted Curriculum & Content Sources

> Starting list from a Deep Research pass. **Confirm the URL, version, and
> license on the source site before relying on or reusing anything** — research

## 1. Philippine curriculum (primary & official)

The system is mid-transition: K to 12 (2013/2016) remains active for several
grades while MATATAG (2023) rolls out in phases (Kindergarten, Grades 1, 4, 7
from SY 2024–2025, continuing through ~2028). Cross-reference both depending on
target grade.

| Title / Publisher | URL & status | Type | Date / version | Curriculum | Coverage | Reliability | Citation & reuse |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| MATATAG Curriculum Guides (DepEd) | deped.gov.ph/matatag-curriculum/ (official) | Primary official | 2023 (rollout 2024–2028) | MATATAG | K–10 (Science, Math, English, Filipino, AP, etc.) | High — definitive official framework. Decongests older K-12 competencies; grade placement of many STEM topics shifted. | Public domain (PH govt work). Free to reuse/quote; attribution required. |
| DepEd LRMDS Portal | lrmds.deped.gov.ph (official) | Primary official | Continuously updated | K to 12 (2016); migrating to MATATAG | Teaching guides, modules, learning competencies (CGs), K–12 | High — official repository. Free account needed to download. Reflects older K-12 until updated. | Educational use permitted; DepEd ©; not for commercialization. |
| General/Subject Shaping Papers (DepEd / ACTRC) | via DepEd MATATAG portal | Primary official | 2023 | MATATAG | Rationale and scope/sequence philosophy, K–10 | High — explains why a topic sits at a given grade. | Public domain. Free to cite/summarize. |

## Identified gaps

- **Araling Panlipunan (Social Studies) & Filipino:** severe lack of openly
  licensed, peer-reviewed interactive digital content. Rely on DepEd LRMDS text
  modules and primary historical/linguistic texts; build visualizers from
  scratch.
- **MATATAG-aligned interactive content:** because MATATAG is new, no major
  third-party platform (including DOST-SEI) has re-mapped to the decongested
  framework yet. Do the cross-walk manually.
